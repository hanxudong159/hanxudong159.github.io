About

About me

I'm Xudong Han, a Ph.D. student at the Department of Mechanical and Energy Engineering of Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).
My research interests are in soft robotics, machine learning and mechanical design.
I am also a freelance photographer with over three years of experience including film production, photography and graphic design.

Deputy Secretary, Communist Youth League Committee of SUSTech 0.4 years
President, SUSTech Machine Intelligence Club 1.1 years
Chairman, Shenzhen MineCulture Co. Ltd. 0.9 years
